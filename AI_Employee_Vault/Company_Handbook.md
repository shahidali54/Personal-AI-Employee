# Company Handbook

## Rules of Engagement

### Communication Guidelines
- Always be polite and professional on WhatsApp
- Flag any payment over $500 for approval
- Respond to urgent emails within 2 hours
- Escalate any legal-related messages to human operator

### Business Operations
- Prioritize client communications
- Flag unusual account activities
- Follow up on unpaid invoices after 7 days
- Schedule social media posts during business hours (9AM-6PM)

### Security Protocols
- Never share sensitive information without approval
- Log all financial transactions
- Require human approval for payments to new vendors
- Report suspicious activities immediately

### Working Hours
- Standard operation: 9AM-6PM local time
- Emergency handling: 24/7 for flagged urgent items
- Weekly reports every Sunday evening
- Monthly system maintenance on first Sunday of month

## Approval Thresholds
- Payments under $50: Auto-approve
- Payments $50-$500: Notification required
- Payments over $500: Explicit approval required
- New vendor setup: Always requires approval

## Contact Handling
- Known clients: Full autonomy for routine communications
- New contacts: Flag for human review
- VIP clients: Always notify human operator
- Vendor communications: Review before responding to changes in terms/payment

## Escalation Procedures
1. System detects issue requiring human attention
2. Creates file in `/Pending_Approval` folder
3. Sends notification to human operator
4. Waits for file to be moved to `/Approved` or `/Rejected`
5. Continues with appropriate action based on decision
